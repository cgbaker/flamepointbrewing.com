First time visiting the Fort Walton Beach Craftathon. This was located at the newly renovated Fort Walton Beach Landing Park. The weather was magnificent and the crowds had a great time. There weren't a lot of homebrewers: just me and the [Escambia County Homebrewers](https://escambiabayhomebrewers.org/). Definitely one to return to!
